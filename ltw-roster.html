HOG

Height:6'0"

Weight:235lbs.

Hometown:RejecKted Outta Hell

HOG spent his early life tending to the animals on the family farm. It was an idyllic life until his mother was killed in mysterious circumstances. These events left HOG damaged, deranged, and vicious. Discovered wearing the face of the family's prized pig, he was little more than a tool of unfocused destruction. Upon meeting sAIN'T, HOG has begun to discover a focus and a purpose. He is now a keen edged weapon bestowed with the power of the Red Rider known as War.

Meat

Height:5'8"

Weight:96lbs.

Hometown:RejecKted Outta Hell

Meat is HOG's uncle and took the loss of his sister in much the same way his nephew did. To this very day Meat hears his sister's disembodied voice from across the void. He drinks heavily to try and quiet her unending talk of woe and despair but no amount of drink can silence the dead. A rail of a man, most of his food is taken in liquid form leaving him an emaciated horror to look upon. Upon meeting Meat, sAIN'T endowed him with the power of the Black Rider known as Famine. His goal is to keep The RejecKts hungry for vengeance and destruction.
